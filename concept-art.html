About this gallery
Art as part of the design process

This collection brings together digital illustrations, character concepts, and visual development work from across my projects and personal practice. Some of it connects directly to other work on this site, like character designs developed for narrative projects. Some of it is purely personal exploration.

Drawing and visual design have been part of how I think through ideas since before I was formally studying game design. Sketching out a character or environment often reveals things about them that writing alone does not.

This gallery will keep growing as I continue developing my visual skills alongside my narrative and game design work.
