About the work
Ongoing freelance for a gaming creator

Since May 2023 I have been designing thumbnails, channel banners, and creator branding assets for Alextreme, a YouTube channel focused on the game Warframe. This is a real ongoing client relationship, not a one-off project.

Thumbnail design for a gaming channel is a specific discipline. The images need to read clearly at very small sizes in a feed, compete visually against a huge amount of other content, and communicate what the video is about in under a second. Over time I have developed a consistent visual language for the channel that balances eye-catching design with recognisable branding.

The work has also covered logos, banners, and other channel branding assets, giving me experience in building and maintaining a coherent visual identity for a creator across multiple formats.
