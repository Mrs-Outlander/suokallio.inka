Who I am

About Me

I'm a game design student at XAMK South-Eastern Finland University of Applied Sciences, specializing in narrative design, world-building, and Unity development. I also hold a Vocational Degree in Game Development (ICT, Gaming Industry), giving me a technical foundation and hands-on experience taking games from concept to playable build.

My focus is on story-driven design, creating worlds, systems, and player experiences where every choice carries weight. I've worked on projects spanning educational Unity games, card game systems, and full D&D campaign world-building, taking on roles from game designer and programmer to 2D artist and visual designer.

Alongside my studies, I freelance as a visual designer for a YouTube content creator, delivering thumbnails, banners, and branding assets for an ongoing real-world client relationship since 2023.
