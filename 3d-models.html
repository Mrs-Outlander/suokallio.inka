About this work
Building a foundation in 3D

These models come from a mix of school assignments and personal practice in Blender. The focus across all of them has been on understanding how 3D tools work, building good habits around topology and UV mapping, and developing an eye for what looks convincing when rendered.

3D modelling feeds directly into my game design work: understanding what is and is not possible in 3D helps me design with real constraints in mind rather than treating the art side as someone else's problem.
